PROMETEO (Nov 2022 – Dec 2024): "Towards Further Integration of Intelligent Robots in Society: Navigate, Recognize and Manipulate".

Focused on improving the integration of mobile and intelligent robots into society. The project targeted three research lines: navigation, recognition and manipulation, aiming to integrate robotic solutions both in natural environments and in close interaction with people.
